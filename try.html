How It Started

Our Dream is Global Learning Transformation

Kawruth was founded by Robert Anderson, a passionate lifelong learner, and Maria Sanchez, a visionary educator. Their shared dream was to create a digital haven of knowledge accessible to all. United by their belief in the transformative power of education, they embarked on a journey to build Kawruth.

Today, Kawruth stands as a thriving educational platform, offering a guided learning path for eager learners, and committed to creating a global community of knowledge seekers.
